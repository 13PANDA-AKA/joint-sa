After 50, daily activity started to feel different — here is what a Cape Town resident did

A Cape Town resident maintaining an active lifestyle after 50.

Cape Town, South Africa. Roughly in his late 50s, John M. began to notice gradual changes in his mobility.

It wasn't sudden. The changes happened slowly — deeper fatigue, stiffness, and more effort required for everyday movements.

Many adults over 50 experience similar changes in mobility but often delay finding support options.

Why mobility can change after 50

Collagen production naturally declines with age. Joint flexibility can decrease. Everyday comfort may gradually change over time.

Instead of ignoring these early signs, more and more South Africans are taking proactive steps to support their long-term mobility.

Quick Self-Assessment

✔️ Is morning stiffness holding you back longer than before?

✔️ Reduced flexibility compared to previous years?

✔️ Requires more effort to climb stairs or take long walks?

If you relate to one or more points above, it is worth considering options for joint support.
